Powered Industrial Truck Inspections

Forklifts and other powered industrial trucks should be inspected before use. Check brakes, steering, horn, lights, forks, mast, tires, hydraulics, seat belt, and warning devices. Remove unsafe equipment from service and report defects. Do not operate equipment with a known safety issue.
