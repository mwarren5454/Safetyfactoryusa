Machine Guarding Basics

Machine guards protect employees from rotating parts, nip points, blades, belts, chains, and moving components. Guards should never be removed, bypassed, modified, or defeated unless an authorized maintenance activity is being performed under proper energy control. If a guard is missing or damaged, stop and report it before operating.
